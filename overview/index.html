A rare architectural gem featuring a semi-rigid composite steel structure that allows for a completely open-concept, column-free floor plan. Built with uncompromising quality, this 5-bedroom residence offers a private spa, professional gym, and bank-level security features. With "Hablex" thermal insulation and premium German interior fittings, this property stands as a benchmark for luxury and durability in the Sajjad area.

Layout & Floor Plan

The architectural flow of this luxury triplex is meticulously designed across three expansive levels to balance grand entertaining with private living. The Basement Level serves as a comprehensive wellness and service hub, featuring a grand entrance lobby, a professional-grade gym, and a sophisticated spa suite equipped with both dry and steam saunas and a jacuzzi. This level also provides secure indoor parking for at least three vehicles, dedicated mechanical rooms, and a fully independent 50 m² staff or janitor suite. Moving to the Ground Floor, the layout transitions into a formal living and dining masterpiece, characterized by a seamless, column-free design and a gourmet main kitchen with direct access to a beautifully landscaped backyard patio. This floor also hosts two spacious master bedrooms and a guest powder room, all accessible via a high-speed elevator. The First Floor is dedicated to the family’s private quarters, offering a cozy secondary lounge, a convenient kitchenette, and three additional bedrooms, including a third master suite. This level is crowned by a magnificent 40 m² semi-covered panoramic terrace, while the fully paved Rooftop remains structurally prepared for a bespoke roof garden or further vertical expansion of up to two additional floors.


Structural & Technical Highlights
  • Structure: Semi-rigid composite steel frame.
  • Architectural Feature: Column-Free Interior (utilizing 9-meter plate girders) for maximum spatial flexibility.
  • Insulation: Double-walled exterior using "Hablex" AAC blocks (Astan Quds brand) for superior thermal and acoustic insulation.
  • Security: 16-channel CCTV system, Bank-grade armored doors, High perimeter walls, and Triple-glazed laminated (shatter-proof) windows.
  • Premium Materials: Beechwood doors, German MDF cabinetry with Blum hardware, and 2cm thick Grade-A Travertine flooring throughout.
